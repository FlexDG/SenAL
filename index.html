Taking Text Analysis to New Heights

SenAl is an easy to use, open source score based sentiment analysis application revolving around AFINN165, a lexical database containing a large group of words that are assigned a positive and negative score. SenAl utilizes Node.js as its main backend foundation accompanied by the logic of a fuzzy decision tree.

Notable Features

Powered by SenAl

  • Sentiment Scoring

    Determine the polarity of a sentence or paragraph through a score based sentiment scoring javascript syntax with the integration of Afinn165 containing lexical strings with different weighted positive and negative values.

  • Comparative Scoring

    The Comparative Score is made available to display the result of the calculation between the total score divided by the total number of lexical words/strings within the sentence/paragraph, improving the accuracy of the final results of polarity.

  • Afinn Transparency

    The lexical strings available which are inputted are displayed alongside their respective values to provide maximum transparency and overall system efficiency to the user.

Spelling Corrections Validation

Spelling corrections will be validated via HTML spellchecker and the Afinn Score. If the words inputted are recorded within Afinn but are not displayed, it means that the user mispelled a word, the mispelled word will then be highlighted by a red marker to alert the user.
